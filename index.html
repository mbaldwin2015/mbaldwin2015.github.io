About Me

Hi! My name's Michael Baldwin, I'm a Full Stack Web Developer and recent Computer Science graduate of Arizona State University. Ever since I was young I had an interest in programming as well as creating my own things. I started by making small flash games and flash websites, playing around with source code, and creating my own tools for video games. Naturally, I decided to major in Computer Science and make programming a career since it is something that I truly enjoy!

I value a broad skill set and I am deeply interested in learning new technologies so that I can become an overall more well rounded programmer. I currently feel like I am a jack of all trades when it comes to Web Development but I am open to specializing and becoming a master of something specific - depends on what the future holds for me, so I am eager to learn new skills on the job or off the job whenever possible!

I'm currently looking for work as either a freelancer or as an employee of a company in the Phoenix, Arizona Metropolitan Area. So if you need some work done or you are a prospective employer interested in interviewing me, please use the contact form below or you can find my contact information in my resume.
